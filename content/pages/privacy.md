## Privacy Policy

*Last updated: February 5, 2026*

Unblocked Games (unblockedgamesg.net) takes your privacy seriously. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our gaming website.

## Information Collection

### Automatically Collected Information

We use Google Analytics to collect the following anonymous information:

- Visit time and duration
- Browser type and version
- Operating system
- IP address (anonymized)
- Games played and time spent
- Device type (desktop/mobile)

### Cookie Usage

Our website uses the following cookies:

- **Google Analytics Cookies**: Used for analyzing website usage and improving user experience
- **Google Ads Cookies**: Used to display relevant advertisements to support our free gaming service

## Information Use

The information we collect is only used for:

- Improving website performance and gaming experience
- Analyzing game popularity and user preferences
- Displaying relevant advertisements to support our free service
- Ensuring games work properly across different devices

## Third-Party Services

### Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to analyze website and game usage. Google Analytics may collect and process your data. For details, please refer to [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

### Google Ads

We use Google Ads to display advertisements that help keep our games free. Google Ads may use cookies to display personalized advertisements. For details, please refer to [Google's Advertising Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads).

## Data Protection

- We do not collect personally identifiable information unless you contact us
- All analytics data is anonymized and aggregated
- We do not sell your information to third parties
- Your game scores and progress are not stored on our servers

## Cookie Management

You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings. However, this may affect the normal functioning of the website and games.

## Policy Updates

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Users will be notified on the website when significant changes occur.
