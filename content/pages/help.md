# Help & FAQ

## Getting Started

Welcome to Unblocked Games! Here's everything you need to know to start playing games on our website.

### How to Play

- Simply click on any game to start playing instantly
- No downloads, registration, or plugins required
- Games work on both desktop and mobile devices
- Use your mouse, keyboard, or touch controls depending on the game

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Do I need to create an account to play games?**
A: No! All games are completely free to play without any registration.

**Q: Why are these games "unblocked"?**
A: Our games are designed to work on restricted networks like schools and workplaces where gaming sites are typically blocked.

**Q: Do the games work on mobile devices?**
A: Yes! Most of our games are mobile-friendly and work great on phones and tablets.

**Q: Are the games safe to play?**
A: Absolutely! All games are carefully selected and safe to play. We only use Google Ads for monetization.

**Q: Why do I see advertisements?**
A: We use Google Ads to support our free service and keep all games free for everyone.

**Q: Can I suggest new games?**
A: Yes! Please contact us through our [contact page](/contact) with your game suggestions.

If you have other questions, please contact us at: **contact@unblockedgamesg.net**
